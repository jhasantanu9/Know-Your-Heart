An interactive web application designed to assess your heart health through a series of questions. The app evaluates your risk of heart disease using a predictive model, providing insights into your heart health and offering tailored advice. Additionally, engage with an AI assistant for further information or heart-related questions. Know Your Heart empowers users to take proactive steps toward better heart health.
